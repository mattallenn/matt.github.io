Emotional Bias: Influence of emotions on one's thoughts and decision making, which can lead to subjective and irrational decisions and judgements. Common emotional biases:

1. Anchoring bias: Being influenced by the first piece of information you've encountered

2. Confirmation bias: Seeking for information that aligns and confirms with preexisting beliefs.

3. Empaty gap bias: Making a decision without considering how we will feel in the future

4. Endowment effect: Assigning something more value than it actually has because we own it.

5. Focusing effect: Focusing too heavily on one aspect of a situation while ignoring others.

6. Framing effect: Choices Influenced by the way information is presented, not the information itself.

There are many more emotional / cognative biases, I will try to cover the malter.
